GOAL

The School aims to provide children with practical hands-on learning experiences that will shape and build their personalities.

MISSION

The Laboratory Elementary School is commited to use every possible venue and opportunity to transform the lives of the pupils under its care by tapping the totality of his human potential in that he may live a full rich life as a responsible member of the home and community.

VISION

The WMSU Laboratory Elementary School envisions itself to be a principal agent in the delivery of quality-basic education.
